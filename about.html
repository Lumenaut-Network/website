What is Lumenaut Network

Lumenaut Network is a collective that are enthusiastic about Stellar.

Lumenaut is a play-on-words based on "lumen" and "naut". Lumen refers to the Stellar's network crypto-asset called Lumens (XLM) and "naut" refers astronaut or cosmonaut. If you like Stellar and hold Lumens (XLM) then you're a lumenaut, just like us.

Members of the Lumenaut Network

The collective is formed from users fomr the official Stellar subreddit, the list is very extense and we don't like to differenciate colaborators. Because we believe that any contribution has its merits we will frequently edit this links with new member.
