What is Lumenaut Network

Lumenaut Network is a collective that are enthusiastic about Stellar.

Lumenaut is a play-on-words based on "lumen" and "naut". The word "lumen" refers to the Stellar's network crypto-asset called Lumens (XLM) and the word "naut" refers to astronaut or cosmonaut. If you like Stellar and hold Lumens (XLM) then you're a lumenaut, just like us.

Members of the Lumenaut Network

The collective is formed from users that meet in the official Stellar subreddit, we don't like to differenciate colaborators because we believe that any contribution has its own merits, because the list may grow we may edit it to add the new members.

As of now all contributors are b1tcc, user, user, user, user, user, user.
