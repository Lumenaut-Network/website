What Is Inflation

Inflation is a nominal mechanism that distributes new Lumens. This event occurs on a weekly basis, at a rate of 1% per year to existing holders. The inflation is distributed using a voting system whereby those with 0.05% of the total votes qualify for the new Lumens. To read more about the inflation process, please consult the official documentation on Stellar.org.

If your address doesn't meet the minimum required votes, then you can still receive the inflation by joining an inflation pool. The inflation pool is a way the community can gather and vote together to reach the minimum required votes. The inflation is paid to the pool and then divided between the members based on account size. Basically, it means that if you're a holder then you receive free Lumens every week!

Requirements of Lumenaut Pool

To receive inflation payments from Lumenaut you need to fulfil one requirement called Lumenaut Minimum Balance. The minimum balance you need to be able to receive inflation payments trough Lumenaut Pool is 2.5 XLM, we don't distribute payments to accounts with less than 2.5 XLM. Lumenaut Minimum Balance requirement is adopted to discourage bots from spamming the network with activity that emerges and disappears while the payments are processed, which repeatedly interrupts or slows the inflation distribution process.

If your address has 2.5 XLM or more, but between the inflation airdrop and the time Lumenaut Pool starts distributing payments you delete your address, or merge it with another address, we will not pay you since your old address doesn't exist in the network. If you want to delete your address but you're voting for Lumenaut Pool, please wait for the inflation payment distribution and then delete your address, because obviously, you will stop receiving payments if your address is deleted.

When Is The Inflation Payment

Inflation is usually distributed by the Stellar network on Tuesdays at 00:00 UTC. Processing on our end should take no more than a few minutes after that. As a result, you should receive your portion of the inflation distribution every Tuesday at around 00:00 UTC.

Voting To Lumenaut Pool

Voting is done by setting the customizable option of Inflation Destination to our address. Your vote is equal to the number of Lumens you hold and the inflation payment you receive is proportionate to the amount of your holdings. The more Lumens you hold - the more Lumens you receive via inflation. Joining Lumenaut Pool doesn't change the fact that you can use your address/wallet like you normally would. Everything is automatic!/p>

Revote of Inflation Destination

Your inflation destination only needs to be set once. The option is tied to your address, not your tokens. So, as long as your address has the Lumens, you will automatically receive your share of the inflation distribution.

Safety of Lumenaut Pool

Inflation destination is a native component of the Stellar network. It is 100% safe because Lumenaut Pool only has access to your Public Key, which is the address you use to vote for us so we can know who you are and send you the inflation payment every week.

Use Of Secret Key To Set Inflation Destination

Every transaction or option set in your address needs to be signed with your Secret Key, to make sure the person issuing the action is the true owner of the address. This process is a good thing, it results in heightened security for your holdings. These are preventative measures to block any third party from setting the inflation destination of your address without your consent.

Depending on the wallet you have and the tutorial you follow - you may need to use your Secret Key to set the inflation destination. For the most part, you will be safe since your Secret Key will be used with your wallet or on the official Stellar website. In case you have trust issues, which is understandable, it's important for you to note that your Secret Key isn't shared with us. We don't need to know your Secret Key and we don't want to. So, there's nothing we can do to damage you or steal your funds.

What is Lumenaut

Lumenaut name is a play-on-words based on “lumen” and “naut”. The word “lumen” refers to the Stellar’s network crypto-asset called Lumen and the word “naut” refers to astronaut or cosmonaut. The collective is formed by users that met in the official Stellar subreddit.

Lumenaut seeks to improve Stellar ecosystem with the new services and continuously improve the existing ones already given by Lumenaut. We don’t develop a service/product already offered by other entity unless we can do something better, this way Stellar benefits from a more healthy ecosystem since we don’t contribute for a saturation in the network.

Lumenaut quickly became a way for users to collectively refer to themselves as a “lumenaut”. If you like Stellar and hold Lumens (XLM) then you’re a lumenaut, just like us.
